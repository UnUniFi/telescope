NFT-Backed Loan

Borrow Tokens on the Security of NFT NFT Management

You can list an NFT and borrow Tokens from a bidder. You can also sell the NFT directly to the bidder.

In case of late repayment, the NFT will be automatically liquidated.
Lend your Tokens to NFT Holder Token Management

You can lend your Tokens at a set interest rate and repayment term. In addition to earning interest rates, you may be able to buy NFTs at lower prices.

The Interest is charged from the time the NFT seller borrows from your deposit.
