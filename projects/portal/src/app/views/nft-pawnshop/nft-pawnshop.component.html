Lend your Tokens to an NFT Holder

What is the Lender?

The lender can lend out tokens at a set interest rate and repayment term. In addition to earning interest, you may have the opportunity to buy NFTs at discounted prices.

The interest begins accruing as soon as the NFT lister chooses to borrow from your deposited tokens.

Set a deposit for NFT bidding and lend tokens to NFT owners.

Borrow Tokens against your Collateralized NFT

What is the Borrower?

You can list an NFT and choose to borrow tokens from the bidder. You can also choose to sell the NFT directly to the bidder.

In the case that loan repayment is late, the NFT will be automatically liquidated.

List NFT, received funds from the bidders and borrow tokens.
