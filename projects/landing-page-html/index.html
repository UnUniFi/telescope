    Use our NFTFi Platform by either listing your NFT on the marketplace or bidding on a listed NFT. Our unique bidding mechanism allows for liquidity aggregation. Listing an NFT collateralizes it, allowing you access flexible loans or simply accept a bid to quickly sell your NFT.
